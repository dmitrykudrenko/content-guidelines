## Перспективні метрики

1. Increase monthly blog traffic by **___**% within the next **___** months.
2. Achieve a customer engagement rate of at least **10%** through comments, social shares, and interactions on blog posts.
3. Generate a **___**% increase in qualified leads through blog content that drives customer interest and engagement.
4. Improve customer satisfaction and loyalty by achieving a customer satisfaction rating of **4 out of 5** for blog content usefulness and relevance (more than **___** votes).
5. Establish thought leadership by securing guest blog contributions from industry experts and influencers at least **once per quarter**.
6. Increase blog subscriber base through targeted email marketing campaigns and blog promotion.
7. Track and analyze key metrics: time spent on page, click-through rates, conversion rates — to continuously optimize blog content and user experience.
8. Ensure that our corporate blog is delivering value to our customers, driving engagement, and contributing to our overall business objectives.
