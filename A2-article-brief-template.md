## Filled Example

```
ARTICLE BRIEF
=============

Title (working): Merge tags and dynamic blocks in Stripo
Date: 2025-02-10
Author: M. Writer
Reviewer: Lead Content Writer

1. PURPOSE & AUDIENCE
   What problem does this article solve for the reader?
   Email marketers struggle to set up personalization beyond basic 
   {{first_name}} — they don't know what dynamic content blocks are, 
   how to configure conditions, or what to show as fallback.

   Target audience (role, experience level):
   Email marketers, 1-3 years experience, familiar with ESPs but 
   new to advanced personalization in email builders.

   What should the reader be able to DO after reading?
   Build an email with 3+ dynamic content blocks using Stripo, 
   with proper fallback values configured.

2. CONTENT TYPE & VI LEVEL
   Content type: How-to Guide (Product-driven)
   Required VI level: 3 (Product content)
   Primary AVI type:  [ ] Systematization  [ ] Unique Knowledge  [x] Product Mastery

   Parent article: n/a (this IS the article)
   Related notes:
   - How merge tags work across ESPs (published 2025-01-05)
   - Building reusable dynamic modules (published 2025-01-18)
   - Fallback values: what to show when data is missing (published 2025-02-01)

3. THESIS CATEGORIES
   Known ideas:
   - Merge tags exist in every ESP
   - Personalization increases engagement

   Intriguing ideas:
   - Most email builders limit dynamic content to text replacement; 
     Stripo supports block-level visibility conditions
   - Fallback strategy is more important than personalization itself

   Unique ideas:
   - Our internal data: emails with 3+ dynamic blocks show 24% higher 
     click-through than single-variable personalization
   - Module-based approach: build once, reuse across campaigns

3. KEY SOURCES
   Link 1: Litmus 2024 State of Email    Why: industry benchmarks on personalization adoption
   Link 2: HubSpot personalization guide  Why: good structure to reference/improve upon
   Link 3: Stripo docs on dynamic content Why: technical accuracy
   Internal data: A/B test results from Q4 2024 (1.2M emails)

5. OUTLINE
   Introduction: "You've added {{first_name}} to your subject line. 
   Now what?" — the gap between basic and advanced personalization.

   Section 1: Merge tags — what they are, how they work, cross-ESP compatibility
   Section 2: Dynamic blocks — visibility conditions, content swapping, Stripo walkthrough
   Section 3: Fallback strategy — what happens when data is missing, default values
   Section 4: Results — our A/B test data, before/after metrics

   Conclusion: 3-step action plan to implement in your next campaign + CTA to try Stripo

6. ENRICHMENT PLAN
   [x] Statistics / data — Litmus 2024 + our internal A/B test (1.2M emails)
   [ ] Expert quotes — reach out to email deliverability expert
   [x] Product examples — dynamic blocks in Stripo (screenshots + video)
   [ ] External case studies — ask customer success for a client example
   [x] Own case studies — Q4 2024 A/B test: +24% CTR with dynamic blocks

7. DISTRIBUTION PLAN
   [x] Blog
   [x] LinkedIn post (key takeaway + 1 stat)
   [x] Email newsletter (teaser + link)
   [ ] Webinar — plan for Q2

   Product accuracy check by:              Date:
```
