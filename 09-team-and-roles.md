## Head of Content

The primary responsibility is coordinating all specialists within the team to achieve the goals of the content strategy. This includes ensuring quality control in content creation and ensuring its alignment with the mission and vision.

Keeping this guideline document in the actual state is also their responsibility.

## Lead Content Writer

Responsible for drafts (others must be accurately rewritten by the Lead).

## Ролі в команді

| Роль | Відповідальність |
|------|-----------------|
| **J. Writer** | Notes, Guest Posts (Rewriting) |
| **SEO specialist** | Finds semantic core for the article |
| **M. Writer** | Article, Guest Posts. Assigns tasks to designer for articles and Whitepapers |
| **S. Writer** | Deeper Investigations, eBook, Checklists, Podcasts, and Webinars. Assigns tasks to designer for presentations, eBook, landing page for the Book |
| **Designer** | All imagery for Notes, Article, SMM, Checklists, Whitepapers, Presentations, eBook (unless freelance) |
| **SMM manager** | Promotes notes, articles, whitepapers; runs surveys; promotes the Book |
| **PR manager** | Promotes articles with guest posts; finds influencers and clients for interviews; ensures influencers share on social media; finds media outlets for promoting the Book |
| **Outreacher** | Finds influencers for interviews |
| **BA** | Helps ask the right questions that will help the company build a better product solution |
