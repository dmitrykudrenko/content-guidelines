# Content Mission & Vision

> Статус: `DRAFT` — перенесено з оригінального документа

## Content Mission

Our content mission is to provide valuable information and resources that empower our customers to make informed decisions and maximize the value of our products. Content messages have to support the product mission and vision.

## Content Vision

Our vision is to be the go-to resource for our audience, offering insights, inspiration, and support that drive their success with our products while establishing our company as a leader in the industry.

## Definition of Valuable Information

> If this content can be generated by ChatGPT, then it is most likely not good content.

3 types of valuable information are necessary for every content to emphasize our expertise:

1. **Fresh Organization (Systematization)** — When we offer our customers a new framework or categorization that enhances their understanding of the world, it holds significant value for them.
2. **Unique Knowledge** — Information previously unfamiliar to our customers, which only we can offer. It showcases our distinct expertise and experience.
3. **Customer Evolution and Product Mastery** — Content dedicated to empowering users to maximize the potential of our product and evolve professionally in their roles. It means educating readers about best practices, advanced features, and strategies that ensure customers are getting the most value from the product.

*There is generally valuable information that is desired to be present.*
