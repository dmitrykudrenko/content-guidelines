# Content Mission & Vision

> Status: `DRAFT` — transferred from original document

## Content Mission

Our content mission is to provide valuable information and resources that empower our customers to make informed decisions and maximize the value of our products. Content messages have to support the product mission and vision.

## Content Vision

Our vision is to be the go-to resource for our audience, offering insights, inspiration, and support that drive their success with our products while establishing our company as a leader in the industry.

## Definition of Valuable Information

> If this content can be generated by ChatGPT, then it is most likely not good content.

Every piece of content must contain at least one of the 3 types of Valuable Information:

1. **Systematization** — a new framework or categorization that enhances the audience's understanding of the topic
2. **Unique Knowledge** — information that only our experience and expertise can provide
3. **Product Mastery** — practical education that helps users maximize the product's potential
