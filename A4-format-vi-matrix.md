## Purpose

Answers the question: "I'm writing a [format] — what VI level do I need and which AVI type fits best?" Maps all 24 publication formats (→ [04-content-types-and-formats.md](04-content-types-and-formats.md)) to VI levels (→ [02-valuable-information.md](02-valuable-information.md)).

## How to Read This Matrix

- **Min VI** — the minimum Valuable Information level required for this format to meet quality standards
- **Recommended AVI** — which type(s) of Additional Valuable Information work best for this format
  - **S** = Systematization (frameworks, categorizations)
  - **U** = Unique Knowledge (our data, insights)
  - **P** = Product Mastery (implementation, how-to)
- **Primary** = the AVI type that should drive the article; **Secondary** = nice to have

## Quick Decision Guide

**"I need to write something fast with minimal depth"** → Formats at VI 0: FAQ, Customer Reviews, Behind-the-Scenes, Interactive Content, Event Recaps, Inspiration, Glossary.

**"I want to establish expertise"** → Formats at VI 2–3 with Systematization: Industry Insights, Research, Comparisons, eBooks, Infographics.

**"I want to show what makes us different"** → Formats with Unique Knowledge primary: Thought Leadership, Case Studies, Myth Busters, Editorials, Our A/B test data.

**"I want to drive product adoption"** → Formats with Product Mastery primary: How-to Guides, Tips, Product Updates, Webinars, Templates.

## Funnel Balance Check

Use this summary to audit your content plan against the ideal 50/30/20 balance (→ [07-creation-principles.md](07-creation-principles.md)):

| Funnel Stage | Formats | Count |
|-------------|---------|-------|
| TOFU (awareness) | 1, 2, 11, 13, 14, 16, 17, 18, 19, 22, 23, 24 | 12 formats |
| MOFU (consideration) | 3, 4, 5, 7, 9, 15, 21 | 7 formats |
| BOFU (decision) | 6, 8, 10, 12, 20 | 5 formats |

If your content plan is heavy on TOFU formats, you'll naturally skew toward the 77/14/9 imbalance. Consciously increase output in MOFU and BOFU formats.
